WELCOME TO THE TIME OF THE RED

THE WORLD BEFORE THE WAR

The Time of the Red is not all that unfamiliar to someone who has lived through the twenty–teens (2013 to 2019) and through the boom–town years of the 2020s. What follows is a brief overview of just a few of the major factors that have shaped the Cyberpunk world since the 4th Corporate War ended and where things stand in the Time of the Red. In the United States, thirty–two years of corrupt government and financial destabilization had resulted in a nation divided—by class, by race, and by economics.
